# Excursion Sales & Cancellation Analysis

> 📍 *This case study is inspired by a real business project I worked on as an Insight Analyst. All data used here has been anonymized or synthetically generated for confidentiality purposes.*

## 🎯 Project Objective

Understand the impact of cancellations on excursion sales and uncover trends and patterns to improve profitability and customer experience.

## 💻 Tools & Technologies

- **Snowflake** – Direct database connection for querying raw data  
- **SQL** – Custom queries to extract and segment relevant data  
- **Power Query** – Data transformation and model building  
- **Power BI** – Interactive dashboards to answer key business questions  

---

## 🕵️‍♀️ Key Business Questions

1. What are the main reasons for cancellations, which excursions are most frequently cancelled, and in which areas?  
2. What is the time-based pattern of cancellations? When and under what conditions do they increase?
3. What is the financial impact of cancellations in terms of revenue loss, and how does this affect sales forecasting?

---

## 📊 Insights Delivered

The dashboard I built helps stakeholders:

- **Reduce cancellations** through data-informed decisions  
- **Minimize operational costs** and improve profitability by addressing root causes  
- **Identify time-based cancellation patterns** to proactively mitigate risks  
- **Estimate customer volume** required to reach sales targets  
- **Optimize regional performance** by adjusting excursion offerings by area  
- **Fine-tune pricing and promotions** based on cancellation trends

---

## ✅ Key Outcomes & Action Plan

- Identified major cancellation triggers and proposed actionable strategies
- Enhanced customer journey by anticipating pain points
- Adjusted excursion offerings using sales + cancellation insights
- Recommended improvements to operational workflows based on data
